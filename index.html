01

Register with your university login

Students sign in via SSO (Microsoft Azure, Google Workspace, or Shibboleth). Businesses create a verified employer account. No paperwork, no back-and-forth.

02

Link your timetable or post your shift

Students grant ShiftMatch permission to read their iCal/Outlook calendar feed — public timetable only, never personal emails. Businesses post a shift in under 60 seconds.

03

Smart matching happens automatically

Shifty checks availability against the shift window and surfaces the right students. Pro+ members get priority vibration alerts first before anyone else.

04

Invite friends & work as a group

After accepting, students see "Invite friends to this shift." Friends check timetable compatibility and tap Join Shift. The employer sees the group as one unit — easy to manage.

05

Clock in, complete the shift, get paid

Students clock in live with a timer. Hours tracked automatically. Timesheets, incident reports and break logs all handled in-app. Pay is reliable and fully transparent.
