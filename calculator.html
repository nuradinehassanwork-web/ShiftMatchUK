How the Charity Pot Works

Simple, transparent, impactful
1
Students & businesses opt in to donate
Completely optional. Students round up earnings, businesses add a monthly pledge.
2
Pot grows throughout the quarter
Every donation is logged in real time. The community can see the pot growing on the website.
3
Charity randomly selected at end of quarter
A verified UK registered charity is selected and the full pot is transferred with proof.
4
Full transparency published
Donation receipt, charity confirmation and impact report shared with the whole community.
